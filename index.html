Places I've lived

I live in Wonder Lake, Illinois. It is a small town just north of McHenry. I have lived here since 2012. I moved in with my sister and lived with her for two years. I moved into the house I live in now in November of 2014. My house is pictured below. It was in need of remodeling. My parents bought it in October and my boys and I could not even move in until a month later. I worked my tail off helping to fix it up and bring up my credit score. In 2017 I brought my credit score up enough so I could get approved for a mortgage loan. I bought my house from my parents and have been paying for it all on my own for 3 years now.

Illinois & Wisconsin

I have lived in only two states. I grew up in McHenry, IL. The first time I moved out I moved into an apartment in Twin Lakes, WI with my sister. We only lived there one year. I moved back to McHenry, IL. I stayed in McHenry for quite some time before moving back to Wisconsin. This time I moved to Burlington. I lived there a couple years before moving to Ringwood, IL. I moved back in with my parents because I was struggling. Once I got back on my feet I moved to Richmond, IL. I got married but things didn't work out. I moved back to Ringwood, IL once again. Finally in 2012 I moved to Wonder Lake,IL where I plan to stay for a very long time.
